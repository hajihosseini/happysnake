Help

Red square: This is food. The score is increased by one.

Blue square: This is food. The score is increased by two.

Pink square: This is food. The score is increased by three.

Green circle :Life is increased by one if it is less than 6.

Black circle :This is a bomb! The score is decreased by five and the life is reduced by one.

Brown block: The score is changed to zero and the life is reduced by one.
